Quadio Scraper

Quadio.com is a music streaming website for college musicians. By capturing data from Quadio, Localify could capture the college music scene, and help young musicians find an audience.

This webscraper was more complex than my Wikipedia Scraper. Quadio is a dynamic, single-page website that requires logging in. In order to build this scraper, I had to use Selenium, a python library.
